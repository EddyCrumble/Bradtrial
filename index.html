AboutMe

Hi I’m Brad Homes. I’ve been a watch enthusiast for over a decade, and that has led to an interest in photography – the process of trying to capture and share what I find interesting and appealing about each and every watch can be quite tough. Writing for several watch blogs has helped me to push further, capturing images from both a documentary and stylistic viewpoint. I’m blessed to have experienced watch from some of the most interesting and exciting new brands.

I haven’t won any awards. I don’t have a vast portfolio of world famous clients. I do have enthusiasm and a love for watches, and you can help me to grow. Watch photography is my main focus, but I’m happy to be challenged! If you’d like to get in touch about working with me please email bradjhomes@gmail.com
